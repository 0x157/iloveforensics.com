There‚Äôs two ways to go about this, one would be to write a quick python script to do the job for us. Another is to use cyberchef, which is more simpler and faster in my opinion.

Here‚Äôs the recipe param for CC: To_Hex('Space',0)Swap_endianness('Hex',16,true)From_Hex('Auto')
